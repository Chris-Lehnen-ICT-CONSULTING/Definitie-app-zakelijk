# US-008: Container Wiring

## Gebruikersverhaal
**As a** developer
**wil ik** validatie services properly wired in the service container
**zodat** dependency injection works correctly

## Acceptatiecriteria

### Criterion 1: Service Registration
**gegeven** the service container initializes
**wanneer** validatie services are needed
**dan** they are properly registered en available

### Criterion 2: Dependency Resolution
**gegeven** validatie service has Afhankelijkheden
**wanneer** the service is requested
**dan** all Afhankelijkheden are correctly injected

### Criterion 3: Singleton Pattern
**gegeven** multiple requests for validatie service
**wanneer** the service is accessed
**dan** the same instance is returned
### Specific
De gebruiker moet exact [ACTIE] kunnen uitvoeren met [RESULTAAT]

### Measurable
Response tijd < 2 seconden, Success rate > 99%, Gebruikerstevredenheid > 8/10


## Technische Notities
- Wiring in `src/services/container.py`
- ModularValidationService registered as singleton
- Afhankelijkheden include rule loader en config
- Lazy initialization for Prestaties
