üéì Lessons Learned

Wat Klopt AAN Team Best Practices:

  1. ‚úÖ **Security is non-negotiable** (API key = disaster)
  2. ‚úÖ **Tests for complex business logic** (45 validation rules)
  3. ‚úÖ **Separation of concerns** (UI vs business logic)
  4. ‚úÖ **Type hints** (solo dev's best friend)

Wat NIET Klopt Voor Solo Dev:

  1. ‚ùå **"Clean architecture" layers** (overhead without team)
  2. ‚ùå **Abstract interfaces** (YAGNI for solo projects)
  3. ‚ùå **100% test coverage** (diminishing returns)
  4. ‚ùå **Comprehensive docs** (you wrote it, you know it)
  5. ‚ùå **Breaking up God Objects** (can make things worse in Streamlit)

The Solo Dev Manifesto:

  1. **Ship features > Perfect architecture**
  2. **Fix what hurts > Fix what's theoretically wrong**
  3. **Pragmatism > Purity**
  4. **Time saved > "Best practices"**
  5. **Simple > Complex**
  6. **Trust yourself** - Your recent performance work proves you know what matters
