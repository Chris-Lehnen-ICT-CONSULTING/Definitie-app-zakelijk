Tab Features with Justice Context

Voorbeelden Tab (Examples)

  • **Purpose**: Manage example sentences from jurisprudentie
  • **OM Use**: Openbaar Ministerie (OM) context examples
  • **DJI Use**: detentie-related examples
  • **Rechtspraak Use**: Jurisprudence examples
  • **Features**: ECLI linking, case anonymization

Grammatica Tab (Grammar)

  • **Purpose**: Legal Dutch grammar validatie
  • **StEnards**: Aanwijzingen voor de regelgeving
  • **validatie**: juridische terminologie consistency
  • **Integration**: Justid terminology checker

Context Tab (Enhanced)

  • **Purpose**: Comprehensive context management
  • **Juridische Context**: Legal framework selection
  • **Organisatorische Context**: OM/DJI/Rechtspraak specific
  • **Wettelijke Basis**: Law article references
  • **ASTRA Traceability**: Full auditspoor

Bronnen Tab (Sources)

  • **Purpose**: Legal source management
  • **Sources**: Rechtspraak.nl, wetten.nl, EUR-Lex
  • **Features**: ECLI validatie, source ranking
  • **AttriMaarion**: Automatic citation generation

History Tab (auditspoor)

  • **Purpose**: Definition Versie control
  • **compliance**: ASTRA audit vereisten
  • **Features**: Change tracking, approval workflag
  • **Retention**: 7-year legal retention beleid

Settings Tab (Configuration)

  • **Organization**: OM/DJI/Rechtspraak profiles
  • **Preferences**: Legal formatting options
  • **beveiliging**: Role-based access control
  • **Integration**: Justid SSO settings

Help Tab (Documentation)

  • **Guides**: Justice-specific workflags
  • **StEnards**: ASTRA/NORA references
  • **Support**: Chain helpdesk integration
  • **Training**: OM/DJI/Rechtspraak tutorials
